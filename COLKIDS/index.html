An Organization working hard to make education accessible by providing Colombian children with new opportunities and skills to grow.

2021

The Issue

Our goal is to establish poor and rural communities in Colombia with educational hubs that provide access to education for kids. This includes having access to teachers, computers and books while ensuring each child’s safety when they learn.

The Break Down

Resources and Materials

20% of donations collected go towards providing kids with resources they can use for references as well as the basic materials each child needs in order to get started.

Student Transportation

We work hard to make sure children can get to and from our facilities safely and with ease. 15% will go to providing student transportation so parents have one less thing to worry about with their children.

Hire staff and Professors

We can have all the books and resources in the world but what good will come from it if there’s no one to teach all these children. 25% of your donations will be used to employ teachers and staff to keep facilities up to date and teach the curriculum.

Infrastructure

40% will be used as the base of our measures. Creating a solid foundation will ensure the longevity of this organization for years to come.
